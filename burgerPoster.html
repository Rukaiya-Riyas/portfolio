Burger Advertisement

Cinematic Food Poster Design

A modern burger advertisement poster concept designed in Photopea, focusing on cinematic food styling, dramatic glow effects, bold typography hierarchy, and promotional social media aesthetics.

Overview

Visual Direction

This self-initiated concept project explored modern food advertisement styling commonly used in restaurant campaigns and social media promotions. The design focused heavily on cinematic lighting, warm contrast, layered composition, and strong visual hierarchy to create a polished commercial aesthetic.

Type

What Was Explored

Typography
Bold condensed typography hierarchy inspired by commercial restaurant advertising and modern delivery campaigns.
Composition

Lighting
Cinematic glow effects and dramatic highlights added to create stronger visual impact and food realism.
Colour Grading
Warm contrast tones and promotional colour styling used to improve appetite appeal and advertisement energy.
