About Me

Design meets Code

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at the Informatics Institute of Technology (affiliated with the University of Westminster), where I channel equal passion into both design thinking and technical implementation.

My sweet spot lives at the intersection of beautiful interfaces and clean code. I don't just design screens, I build them. Whether I'm crafting high-fidelity prototypes in Figma or writing React components with Tailwind CSS, I bring the same obsessive attention to user experience throughout.
